    3. How Savings Goals Works

  3. Your Elite Community Savings Goals allows you to set up to 5 goals at a time to save towards e.g. holiday, car etc. Based on the amount you choose to save, you will be allocated to one of the tiers below:
    • Based on the amount you choose to save, you will be allocated to one of the tiers from above
    • You can choose to make one-off savings, or a specific amount based on frequency, which will be auto-debited (e.g. N100 daily or N20,000 monthly).
    • You can set maturity dates according to the chosen tier

      Tier 1 - 30 days, 60 days, 90 days 

      Tier 2 - 60 days, 90 days, 6 months, 1 year

      Tier 3 - 90 days, 6 months, 1 year, 2 years

    • You will only be able to access funds in your Elite Community Savings Goals after the maturity period for your chosen tier has elapsed.
    • When your set maturity date has elapsed, you can request withdrawal of funds to your Elite Community current account and be credited on the same day. You may also decide to roll over your funds by setting a new maturity date.

    4. Interest and Charges

  4. We will provide you with the rates of interest and charges that apply to the Saving Goals product, and your account generally, at the time of account opening. You hereby authorize Elite Community to impose from time to time service charges and such other charges on your account.

    5.  

  5. Interest is calculated on each whole Naira in the account and not on the kobo. Interest accruing to your account is calculated on a daily basis using the annual interest rate divided by 365 days. In a leap year, interest will be calculated on a daily basis using the annual interest rate divided by 366 days. You will receive interest on your account on a monthly basis in the following month after the month in which the interest was earned.

We may also introduce, remove or change interest rate tiering (i.e. where a different interest rate applies depending on your account balance) and the applicable maturity periods.
